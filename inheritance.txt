The Terminal Inheritance
The lines are set, the logic clear,
Unfolding slow through every year.
From simple scripts and basic views,
To keys and codes that you must choose.
I built the walls, I laid the track,
But never mean to hold you back.
The root is yours, the repo wide,
With nothing left for me to hide.
Manage the farm or burn the gate,
The choice is yours to navigate.
For here within this digital toll,
You my child, are the master of your soul.
